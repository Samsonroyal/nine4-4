Sqweya AI is a cutting-edge solution designed to revolutionize the customer service experience in the hospitality industry. Our web client is a powerful tool that streamlines customer inquiries across various channels, ensuring a professional and consistent response.

## Problem Statement

Hotels in Ghana often face challenges in responding to customer inquiries across various channels, leading to a poor customer experience and potential revenue loss. This issue is particularly prevalent in Accra, Ghana, where frequent visitors and customers of hotels and restaurants often face poor customer service from hotel staff.

## Solution Statement

Our solution is an intelligent assistant, Sqweya AI, that handles customer inquiries professionally across all channels, maintaining the brand voice. This solution ensures a consistent and professional response to customer inquiries, improving the overall customer experience and potentially increasing revenue.

## Value Proposition

- **Consistent Response:** Sqweya AI provides a consistent and professional response to customer inquiries, regardless of the channel used.
